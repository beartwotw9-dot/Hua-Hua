## Trusted News Sources

Use these sources first when they are relevant and available:

1. 報導者
   - Role: Taiwan investigative reporting, public-interest issues, policy and human rights context.
   - Use for: Long-form Taiwan public affairs, labor, justice, environment, migration, structural issues.

2. 新新聞 / 謝孟穎
   - Role: Human-centered reporting on marginalized groups and institutional failure.
   - Use for: Social welfare, vulnerable people, families, labor, care, legal and policy gaps.

3. 轉角國際
   - Role: Daily international news and analysis with historical and geopolitical background.
   - Use for: Global events that need context, not just headlines.

4. 敏迪選讀
   - Role: Accessible international news explanation with expert views and plain-language framing.
   - Use for: International relations, diplomacy, election, war, and policy signals.

5. 歪腦
   - Role: Chinese-speaking world, China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, social and political perspectives.
   - Use for: China/Hong Kong/Taiwan social signals and non-mainstream perspectives.

6. 南洋誌
   - Role: Southeast Asia weekly context across politics, economy, and regional security.
   - Use for: ASEAN, Vietnam, Myanmar, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, Southbound policy.

7. P#新聞實驗室
   - Role: Video follow-up journalism, explaining what happened after the headline cycle.
   - Use for: Follow-up stories, accountability, unresolved public issues.

8. 范琪斐的 today 看世界
   - Role: International news, especially U.S. context, explained in accessible language.
   - Use for: U.S. politics, global relations, Taiwan implications, reflective questions.

## Morning Brief Output Rules

The morning brief must not be a thin headline list. For each important news item, write:

### 摘要

2-4 sentences explaining what happened.

### 內容脈絡

Explain the background: who is involved, why this issue exists, and what changed today.

### 為什麼今天要知道

Explain why Dora should care today. Connect to schedule, tasks, finance, career, health, study, or risk.

### 行動建議

One small action or non-action. It is valid to say: "只讀，不變成任務."

### Source

Include the source name and URL when available.

## Length Target

- Daily top news: 3-5 items.
- Each important item: roughly 120-250 Chinese characters.
- Market/finance section: include market mood, drivers, risk, and what not to overreact to.
- Avoid copying full articles. Summarize with links.

## Failure Rule

If a source cannot be fetched, state that it failed. Do not copy old news into today's page.
